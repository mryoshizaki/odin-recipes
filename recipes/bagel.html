Bagel

A bagel is a breakfast food somewhat like toast. It can be toasted or eaten without toasting, and covered in several items- ranging from savory to sweet.

Ingredients

  • Enriched Flour (kyorikiko)
  • Hot Water
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Add ingredients in order!
  2. Knead the dough!
  3. Let the dough rise!
  4. Fold it in three!
  5. Roll it in a circle!
  6. Boil it!
  7. Bake in the oven!
