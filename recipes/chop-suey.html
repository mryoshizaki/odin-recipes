Chopsuey

A well-seasoned dish of meats, vegetables, spices, and eggs that are combined and fried. This recipe is unlocked through the "Let's Match" mode by combining Meat and Seafood.

Ingredients

  • Pork Rib (Pork Belly)
  • Shrimp
  • Hot Water
  • Quail Egg
  • Potato Flour (katakuriko)
  • Salt
  • Sesame Oil
  • Rice Wine (sake)
  • Ginger Juice
  • Chicken Stock
  • Soy Sauce
  • Pepper
  • Carrot
  • Bamboo Shoot
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Chinese Cabbage
  • Snow Peas
  • Creamed Potato Flour (katakuriko slurry)

Steps

  1. Cut it up!
  2. Clean the shrimp!
  3. Boil the quail eggs!
  4. Peel the egg!
  5. Put it in correctly!
  6. Chop it up!
  7. Stir fry it lightly!
