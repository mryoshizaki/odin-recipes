Grilled corn

Corn is a sweet vegetable with yellow kernels found in many dishes, main or side. Grilling it on the cob is a popular way of serving corn. Although in cooking, it is used as a vegetable, botanically, it is actually a grain, being able to make cornmeal and corn starch.

Ingredients

  • Corn
  • Tare Sauce

Steps

  1. Peel the skin!
  2. Time it right!
  3. Grill it!
